02

Operator + consultant + builder — at senior level in each role

Most people are either a consultant who advises operators, or an operator who hires consultants. Volodymyr has been both — and has also built businesses from scratch (growing a consulting practice to €13M, scaling a country operation to 300+ people and $120M in contracts). That three-way experience eliminates blind spots that specialists on either side typically carry.

The Heriot-Watt MSc was completed in 2021 — ahead of the energy transition wave. Quantitative rigour applied to strategy — not just narrative

The computer science foundation enabled building proprietary market projection tools that underpinned SLB's 5-year corporate strategy — not outsourcing the modelling to analysts, but owning it. This data-first instinct, rare among MBAs, means strategic recommendations come with defensible numbers rather than slide-deck intuition.

05

Truly international — culturally and linguistically

Fluent in four languages (English, French, Ukrainian, Russian), with hands-on commercial experience across Europe, Middle East and Africa. France-based and internationally mobile. In markets where trust is built in the local language, this is a structural competitive advantage that cannot be acquired in a training course.
